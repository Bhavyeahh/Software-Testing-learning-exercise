Expected result: entry is blocked with validation error.\\

Modules: Registration, Crew Management, Race Management.\\
Expected result: registered member with non-driver role cannot enter race.\\
Actual result: PASS.\\
Errors/issues found: None.\\
Why needed: verifies role-based eligibility constraints.

Modules: Race Management, Results, Inventory.\\
Expected result: rankings update and prize money is reflected in inventory cash.\\
Actual result: PASS.\\
Errors/issues found: None.\\
Why needed: confirms results-to-inventory data flow.

Modules: Mission Planning, Registration/Crew Management, Inventory.\\
Expected result: mission cannot start for damaged car without mechanic availability.\\
Actual result: PASS.\\
Errors/issues found: None.\\
